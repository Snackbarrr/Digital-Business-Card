About

AlwaysAnotherAngle is a media journal by Yusrizalakbar — a raw gallery and commentary on daily life. Whether it’s an ordinary afternoon, a journey to foreign places, or a collaboration with other creators, AlwaysAnotherAngle, through film and photography, provides a fresh perspective: a new idea, a different method, a unique angle on the moments we all encounter. This space is dedicated to sharing those reflections and stories. Over time, you’ll find a timeline of growth, learning, creativity, and connection.
