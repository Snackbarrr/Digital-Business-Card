About

Always Another Angle is a media project by Yusrizal Akbar Junaedi. It’s built on a belief that perspective changes everything, life moves fast and we often miss out on details. Through analogue film and digital photography, personal projects and commercial partnerships, we invite a second look at the everyday — the familiar things we see but not noticed.

It’s not about loud branding. The value lies in the angle you take — in how you choose to see.
