About

Always Another Angle is a media project by Yusrizal Akbar Junaedi. It’s built on a simple belief: that perspective changes everything. Through film, photography, and clothing, the project invites a second look at the everyday the familiar things we pass by without noticing.

It’s not about loud branding. The value lies in the angle you take in how you choose to see.
